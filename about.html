About Me

Hello! I'm a first-year BSIT student at Adamson University, deeply passionate about coding and technology. Ever since I started programming, I've enjoyed exploring different aspects of software development, from building interactive websites to creating game mechanics.

Whether it's working on a big project or simply coding for fun, I find joy in problem-solving and pushing my skills further. Coding, to me, isn't just about writing lines of code—it's about creativity, innovation, and making ideas come to life.

In my free time, I work on personal coding projects, especially in game development—primarily on Roblox Studio, where I enjoy scripting mechanics and designing immersive experiences.
